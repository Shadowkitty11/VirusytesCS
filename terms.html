MYO Approvals

  1. Before uploading your design to a slot, you MUST get them approved from the Virusytes account, TH, or discord server.

  3. All MYO approval must include: Link to slot, Rarity, detailed list of traits, any item add-ons, and an image of the design.

Ownership Transfers

  1. To transfer to a different user, you must let us know before hand either in the dedicated "Masterlist Updates" section in our TH or Discord. It will be transferred by an admin ASAP.
  2. Upon trading, reselling or gifting the design to another individual, the previous owner agrees to transfer all ownership rights to the recipient.

Design Voiding

  1. If you wish to void a slot or designed Virusyte, please make sure you let us know via the @virusytes_ML account
  2. The voided design will still remain on the masterlist for future reference.
  3. If you wish to still keep the design but separate it from the species, we ask then you re-attach limbs, remove the corrupt eye, and change the parasite tail to distance from the species.
  4. If the design was created by you: You have full rights to the design and can do whatever you'd like with it.
  5. If the design was created by someone else: The design will follow the original designer's TOS.
  6. Designs listed as "OFFICAL" will NOT be able to be voided
